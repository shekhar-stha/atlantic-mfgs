About US

Atlantic Manufacturing Solutions (AMS)

Atlantic Manufacturing Solutions (AMS) is a South Florida-based manufacturing/engineering consulting firm specializing in the design, development, and fabrication of mechanical parts and assemblies. AMS offers turnkey services such as product development, machine design, manufacturing optimization, prototype fabrication/testing, manufacturer sourcing, and more. AMS is ready to bring your next idea to market, solve your engineering challenges, or help improve your existing products.
